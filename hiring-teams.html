What I Do

I work with engineering teams that want thoughtful visibility into early-career talent and clearer alignment around internships and entry-level roles. Through my engineering mentor channels, I help teams share role context, set expectations, and reach motivated early-career engineers who are actively preparing for hiring cycles.

What This Looks Like

  • Curated early-career spotlights that help talent get seen by the right teams
  • Role and team visibility that reaches motivated interns and new grads
  • Clarifying expectations so teams attract better-fit candidates
  • Helping reduce noise by reaching candidates already engaged in career development

What This Is Not

  • Not recruiting, staffing, or placement
  • Not a guarantee of interviews, offers, or hires
  • No resume forwarding or candidate representation
  • No negotiation involvement

Best Fit

This is a good fit for teams that hire interns or entry-level engineers and want a practical way to improve visibility and alignment with early-career expectations without turning hiring into a volume game.

How Teams Engage

  • One-off support during a specific internship or entry-level hiring cycle
  • Short-term collaboration during peak recruiting periods
  • Ongoing visibility for teams that hire early-career engineers year-round
